<p><p>The feldspathic minerals market primarily includes two types: plagioclase feldspar and potassium feldspar. Plagioclase feldspar is a solid solution series ranging from sodium-rich to calcium-rich varieties, commonly used in the production of ceramics, glass, and as fillers. Potassium feldspar, primarily comprising orthoclase, is valued for its chemical properties and is widely used in ceramics, glass, and industrial applications. Both types serve essential roles in construction materials, glass production, and various industrial processes, driving their market demand.</p></p>
