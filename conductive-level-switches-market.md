<p><p>Conductive level switches are essential devices used to detect the presence or absence of liquids in various applications. The market for these switches can be categorized into two main types: integral and split. Integral type switches combine the sensor and electronics into a single unit, offering simplicity and ease of installation. In contrast, split type switches separate the sensor from the electronics, allowing for installation in challenging environments and facilitating maintenance while providing greater flexibility in applications.</p></p>
