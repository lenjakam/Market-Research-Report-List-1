<p><p>The Vehicle Anti-Theft System market includes various technologies designed to prevent vehicle theft. Alarm systems alert owners and bystanders to unauthorized access. Immobilizers disable the engine without the correct key, hindering theft. Steering locks physically secure the steering wheel, making vehicles difficult to steer. Passive keyless entry allows access without traditional keys, while central locking systems secure all doors simultaneously. Biometric capture devices enhance security by using unique biological traits for authentication, providing advanced theft protection.</p></p>
