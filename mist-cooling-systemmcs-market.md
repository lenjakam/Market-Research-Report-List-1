They are categorized by pressure levels: Low Pressure MCS operates at around 100 psi, ideal for small spaces; Medium Pressure MCS functions between 100-300 psi, suitable for larger areas; and High Pressure MCS, operating above 300 psi, provides efficient cooling for extensive outdoor areas while minimizing water usage. Each type enhances comfort and efficiency, catering to diverse applications in residential, commercial, and industrial settings.</p></p>
