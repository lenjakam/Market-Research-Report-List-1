<p><p>Refractive Index Detectors (RID) are essential in chromatography, particularly in High-Performance Liquid Chromatography (HPLC) and Size Exclusion Chromatography (SEC). In HPLC, RIDs provide precise concentration measurements of compounds, enhancing the separation process. In SEC, they help in analyzing polymers and biomolecules by detecting size variations based on refractive index changes. Both applications benefit from RIDs' sensitivity and ability to handle a wide range of analytes, making them valuable tools in various chemical and pharmaceutical analyses.</p></p>
