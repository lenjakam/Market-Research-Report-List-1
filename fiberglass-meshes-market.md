<p><strong>The Fiberglass Meshes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Interior Fiberglass Mesh</li><li>External Fiberglass Mesh</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fiberglass meshes market is divided into two primary types: interior fiberglass mesh and external fiberglass mesh. Interior fiberglass mesh is designed for applications within buildings, providing support and reinforcement to drywall, plaster, and ceilings while enhancing thermal insulation and fire resistance. In contrast, external fiberglass mesh is used for exterior applications, such as stucco, EIFS (Exterior Insulation and Finish Systems), and siding, offering durability against weather elements and promoting structural integrity. Both types serve essential roles in construction and renovation projects.</p></p>
