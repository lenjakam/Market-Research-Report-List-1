<p><strong>The UV Stabilizers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>HALS (Hindered Amine)</li><li>UV Absorber</li><li>Quenchers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The UV stabilizers market comprises three main types: Hindered Amine Light Stabilizers (HALS), UV absorbers, and quenchers. HALS are effective in scavenging free radicals, prolonging the life of polymers. UV absorbers absorb harmful UV radiation, converting it into heat and preventing degradation. Quenchers, including various additives, dissipate energy from UV exposure, reducing the likelihood of material breakdown. Each type plays a crucial role in enhancing the durability and performance of materials used in outdoor applications and products exposed to sunlight.</p></p>
