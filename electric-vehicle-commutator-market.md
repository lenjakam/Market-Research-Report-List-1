<p><strong>The Electric Vehicle Commutator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hook Type Commutator</li><li>Groove Commutator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric vehicle commutator market is primarily segmented into two types: hook type commutators and groove commutators. Hook type commutators feature hooks that secure the winding wires, ensuring better connectivity and durability in high-performance applications. In contrast, groove commutators have grooves along their surface, designed to provide a more efficient path for electrical current, promoting smoother operation. Both types cater to different technical requirements, influencing their usage in various electric vehicle designs and performance levels.</p></p>
