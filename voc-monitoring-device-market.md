<p><strong>The VOC Monitoring Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PID</li><li>Metal-oxide Semiconductor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The VOC (Volatile Organic Compounds) monitoring device market encompasses various technologies, primarily focusing on PID (Photoionization Detector) and metal-oxide semiconductor sensors. PID sensors utilize ultraviolet light to ionize VOCs, offering high sensitivity and fast response times, making them ideal for real-time monitoring in various environments. Metal-oxide semiconductor sensors operate by detecting changes in resistance caused by VOC interactions with a chemical coating, providing cost-effective and compact solutions suitable for indoor air quality applications. Both technologies cater to diverse industries, including environmental monitoring and occupational safety.</p></p>
