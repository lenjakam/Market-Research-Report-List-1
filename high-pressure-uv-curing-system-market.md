<p><ul><li>Spot Cure</li><li>Flood Cure</li><li>Focused Beam</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The High Pressure UV Curing System market includes three main types: Spot Cure, Flood Cure, and Focused Beam. Spot Cure systems deliver concentrated UV light to specific areas, ideal for precise applications like electronics. Flood Cure systems emit a broad UV beam, covering larger surfaces, making them suitable for coatings and adhesives. Focused Beam systems combine elements of both, providing targeted curing with adjustable intensity, allowing for versatility in various manufacturing processes. Each type addresses unique curing requirements across industries.</p></p>
