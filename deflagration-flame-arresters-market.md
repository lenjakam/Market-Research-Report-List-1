<p><p>Deflagration flame arresters are safety devices designed to prevent the propagation of flames in process piping and equipment. The market for these devices can be categorized into two main types: in-line and end-of-line. In-line arresters are installed within pipelines, allowing for continuous flow while ensuring flame containment. Conversely, end-of-line arresters are placed at the terminal points of systems, providing protection against backflash into storage tanks or equipment. Both types are essential for mitigating explosion risks in industrial applications.</p></p>
