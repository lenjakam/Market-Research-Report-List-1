<p><p>The intelligent smoke detector market features several key types: </p><p>1. **Photoelectric** detectors use a light source and sensor to identify smoke particles, making them effective for slower, smoldering fires. </p><p>2. **Dual Sensors** combine both photoelectric and ionization technologies for enhanced detection versatility. </p><p>3. **Ionization** detectors respond quickly to fast-burning fires but can be slower with smoldering ones. </p><p>4. **Others** include advanced models incorporating smart technology, connectivity features, and integration with home automation systems, enhancing user awareness and safety.</p></p>
