<p><p>Ultra High Performance Concrete (UHPC) is a class of advanced concrete characterized by its superior strength, durability, and versatility. It encompasses various types, including Slurry-infiltrated Fibrous Concrete (SIFCON), which enhances toughness and impact resistance; Reactive Powder Concrete (RPC), known for its high compressive strength and low permeability; and Compact Reinforced Composite (CRC), which combines fibers and aggregates for improved structural performance. Each type serves specialized applications, offering innovative solutions for demanding construction needs in both infrastructure and architectural projects.</p></p>
