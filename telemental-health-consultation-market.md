<p><p>Telemental health consultation encompasses various therapy types tailored for remote delivery. Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) focuses on changing negative thought patterns to improve emotional regulation. Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT) combines cognitive and behavioral techniques with mindfulness, aiding emotional control and interpersonal effectiveness. Interpersonal Psychotherapy addresses relationship issues to enhance emotional functioning. Additionally, the "Others" category includes diverse therapies like Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT) and Mindfulness-Based Stress Reduction (MBSR), offering flexible options for diverse mental health needs in a remote setting.</p></p>
