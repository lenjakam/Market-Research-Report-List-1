<p><ul><li>Modified Asphalt</li><li>Impregnated Asphalt</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The asphalt binder market consists of various types, including modified asphalt and impregnated asphalt. Modified asphalt is enhanced with additives, improving durability, flexibility, and temperature resilience, making it ideal for high-stress applications. Impregnated asphalt, on the other hand, involves saturating porous materials with liquid asphalt, resulting in improved waterproofing and strength. Both types cater to different construction needs, thereby expanding their applications in road construction, roofing, and maintenance, contributing to the overall growth of the asphalt binder market.</p></p>
