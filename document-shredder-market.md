<p><p>The document shredder market consists of various types, primarily including strip-cut and cross-cut shredders. Cross-cut shredders provide a higher level of security by cutting paper into small squares, making it nearly impossible to reconstruct the original document. Other types may include micro-cut shredders for maximum security and industrial shredders for bulk processing, catering to different privacy needs and shredding capacities.</p></p>
