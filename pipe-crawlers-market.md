<p><ul><li>SCR</li><li>Rigid</li><li>Flexible</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pipe crawlers market is categorized into three main types: SCR (Self-Contained Remote), rigid, and flexible crawlers. SCR crawlers are autonomous and equipped with their own power source, making them suitable for various environments. Rigid crawlers feature a sturdy design, ideal for straight pipelines and tough conditions, while flexible crawlers are designed to navigate bends and curves in pipes. Each type offers unique advantages, catering to different inspection and maintenance needs within the pipeline industry.</p></p>
