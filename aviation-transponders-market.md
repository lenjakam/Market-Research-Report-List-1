<p><p>Aviation transponders are crucial for aircraft identification and tracking. Response Mode A transponders provide a basic identification signal to air traffic control. Response Mode B adds altitude information, enhancing situational awareness. Response Mode C offers advanced capabilities by transmitting pressure altitude data, allowing for improved traffic separation and safety. Each mode serves its function within the airspace system, catering to varying requirements for surveillance and communication in both commercial and general aviation sectors, thus influencing the transponder market dynamics.</p></p>
