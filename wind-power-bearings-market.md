<p><p>The wind power bearings market includes three primary types: Main Shaft Bearings, which support the rotor and transmit loads; Yaw Bearings, which enable the turbine to rotate and align with prevailing winds; and Variable Pitch Bearings, which allow for adjustments in blade angle to optimize performance and energy capture. Additionally, Accelerating Engine Bearings are crucial for managing the thrust generated during turbine start-up and operation. Each type plays a vital role in enhancing wind turbine efficiency and reliability.</p></p>
