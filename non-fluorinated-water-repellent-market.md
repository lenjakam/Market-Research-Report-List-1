<p><p>The non-fluorinated water repellent market primarily consists of two types: solvent-based and water-based products. Solvent-based repellents utilize organic solvents to create a protective barrier against water, often providing enhanced durability and performance in harsh conditions. In contrast, water-based repellents leverage water as a solvent, offering easier application, reduced toxicity, and lower environmental impact. Both types are gaining traction due to increasing demand for eco-friendly alternatives in industries like textiles, construction, and automotive, catering to sustainability trends.</p></p>
