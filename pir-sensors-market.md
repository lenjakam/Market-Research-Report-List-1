<p><ul><li>Analog PIR Sensors</li><li>Digital PIR Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PIR sensor market is divided into two main types: analog and digital. Analog PIR sensors provide continuous voltage readings that are proportional to detected infrared radiation, making them simpler but less versatile. In contrast, digital PIR sensors convert infrared signals into digital data, enabling more advanced features like programmable settings and enhanced signal processing. This digital capability allows for better performance in complex environments, leading to increased applications in security, automation, and energy management systems.</p></p>
