<p><strong>The Veterinary Electrosurgical Units Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monopolar Veterinary Electrosurgical Units</li><li>Bipolar Veterinary Electrosurgical Units</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The veterinary electrosurgical units (ESUs) market is primarily divided into two types: monopolar and bipolar units. Monopolar veterinary electrosurgical units use a single active electrode to cut and coagulate tissue, requiring a grounding pad for electrical completion. They are versatile and commonly used for a variety of surgical procedures. In contrast, bipolar veterinary electrosurgical units feature two electrodes that work together, allowing for precise control and reduced thermal spread, making them ideal for delicate surgeries and minimizing damage to surrounding tissues.</p></p>
