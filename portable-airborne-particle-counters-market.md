<p><p>The Portable Airborne Particle Counters market is categorized into ionizing and non-ionizing types based on their detection methods. Ionizing particle counters utilize radiation to charge particles, allowing for accurate detection of small particles, making them ideal for environments requiring strict contamination control, like pharmaceuticals. Non-ionizing counters, on the other hand, use light scattering techniques to measure particle concentration, suitable for general air quality monitoring. Both types are essential for ensuring clean air standards in various industries.</p></p>
