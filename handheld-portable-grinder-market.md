<p><p>The handheld portable grinder market encompasses three main types: pneumatic, electric, and hydraulic. Pneumatic grinders utilize compressed air for power, offering greater mobility and lightweight design, making them ideal for industrial applications. Electric grinders rely on electrical power and are widely used for their versatility and convenience across various tasks. Hydraulic grinders are driven by hydraulic fluid, providing high torque and efficiency, suitable for heavy-duty operations. Each type serves distinct needs in both professional and DIY settings.</p></p>
