<p><p>The Invasive Blood Glucose Meter market is segmented based on measurement technology. GDH-FAD (Glucose Dehydrogenase Flavin Adenine Dinucleotide) offers high specificity for glucose, ensuring accurate results. GOD (Glucose Oxidase) utilizes an enzyme-based approach but may be affected by other sugars. Electrochemistry encompasses various techniques for detecting glucose levels electrically, enhancing precision. GDH (Glucose Dehydrogenase) also uses enzymatic methods, focusing on glucose. The "Other" segment includes alternative technologies and innovations not categorized elsewhere, reflecting diverse market advancements.</p></p>
