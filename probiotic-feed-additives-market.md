<p><p>Probiotic feed additives are available in two primary forms: liquid and solid. Liquid probiotics are typically offered in a suspension, making them easy to mix with feed or water, promoting rapid uptake by animals. They are often used in intensive livestock operations where precise dosage is crucial. Solid probiotics, on the other hand, come in powder or pellet form, allowing for straightforward incorporation into feed during manufacturing. Both types aim to enhance gut health, digestion, and overall animal performance, catering to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
