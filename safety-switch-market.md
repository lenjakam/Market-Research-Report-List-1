<p><ul><li>Contact Switch</li><li>Non-contact Switch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The safety switch market is primarily divided into two types: contact switches and non-contact switches. Contact switches operate by physically engaging with the equipment, providing a reliable disconnection method that ensures safety during maintenance. In contrast, non-contact switches utilize sensors, detecting the presence or absence of an object without physical contact. This type enhances safety by minimizing wear and tear and reducing the risk of mechanical failure. Both types are crucial in various industries for ensuring safe machinery operation and compliance with safety standards.</p></p>
