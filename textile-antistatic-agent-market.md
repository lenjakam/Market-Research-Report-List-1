<p><ul><li>Anion</li><li>Cation</li><li>Zwitterion</li><li>Nonionic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The textile antistatic agent market comprises four main types: anionic, cationic, zwitterionic, and nonionic agents. Anionic agents carry a negative charge and effectively reduce static buildup, while cationic agents possess a positive charge, enhancing conductivity in different textiles. Zwitterionic agents feature both positive and negative charges, offering versatile static control suitable for various fabrics. Nonionic agents, which have no charge, are compatible with diverse textile materials and environmentally friendly, providing effective static reduction while minimizing chemical interactions.</p></p>
