<p><p>The Gas-insulated Circuit Breakers (GIS) market is primarily categorized into AC and DC segments. AC GIS are designed for alternating current applications, providing enhanced performance and compactness, ideal for urban environments. DC GIS, on the other hand, cater to direct current systems, often used in renewable energy applications and high-voltage direct current (HVDC) transmissions. Both types benefit from a gas insulation medium, offering insulation and arc-extinguishing capabilities while requiring less space compared to traditional air-insulated breakers.</p></p>
