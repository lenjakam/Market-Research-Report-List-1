<p><strong>The Nuclear Reactor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pressurized water reactor (PWR)</li><li>Boiling water reactor (BWR)</li><li>Pressurized heavy water reactor (PHWR)</li><li>Gas-cooled reactor (AGR & Magnox)</li><li>Fast neutron reactor (FBR)</li><li>Light water graphite reactor (RBMK & EGP)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nuclear reactor market comprises various types, each serving distinct purposes. Pressurized Water Reactors (PWR) utilize high-pressure water to transfer heat, while Boiling Water Reactors (BWR) boil water to produce steam directly. Pressurized Heavy Water Reactors (PHWR) use heavy water for moderation. Gas-cooled reactors like Advanced Gas-cooled Reactors (AGR) and Magnox employ gas for cooling. Fast Neutron Reactors (FBR) utilize high-energy neutrons for efficient fuel use, while Light Water Graphite Reactors (RBMK & EGP) combine graphite moderation with light water coolant. Each type caters to specific energy needs and regional preferences.</p></p>
