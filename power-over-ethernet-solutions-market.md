<p><strong>The Power Over Ethernet Solutions Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Power Sourcing Equipment Controllers & ICs</li><li>Powered Device Controllers & ICs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Power Over Ethernet (PoE) Solutions market comprises two main types: Power Sourcing Equipment (PSE) Controllers & ICs and Powered Device (PD) Controllers & ICs. PSE Controllers manage power delivery from sources like switches or injectors to connected devices, ensuring efficient energy distribution. PD Controllers, on the other hand, are integrated into devices receiving power, enabling them to negotiate power levels and manage consumption safely. Together, these components facilitate seamless power and data transmission over Ethernet, enhancing device connectivity and functionality.</p></p>
