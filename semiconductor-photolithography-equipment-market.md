<p><p>Semiconductor photolithography equipment is essential for chip manufacturing, utilizing various light types to create intricate circuit patterns. The UV (Ultraviolet) market involves traditional photolithography using short wavelengths for larger feature sizes. DUV (Deep Ultraviolet) employs shorter wavelengths to achieve finer resolutions, pivotal for advanced technology nodes. EUV (Extreme Ultraviolet) utilizes even shorter wavelengths, enabling the production of extremely small features, essential for next-generation semiconductors. The market is driven by the ongoing demand for miniaturization and enhanced performance in electronic devices.</p></p>
