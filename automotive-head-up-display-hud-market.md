<p><ul><li>Windshield Projected HUD</li><li>Combiner Projected HUD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive Head-up Display (HUD) market is primarily divided into two types: Windshield Projected HUD and Combiner Projected HUD. Windshield Projected HUD projects information directly onto the vehicle's windshield, allowing drivers to access vital data without looking away from the road. This enhances safety and convenience. In contrast, Combiner Projected HUD involves a compact display that projects information onto a transparent screen positioned in front of the driver. Both types aim to improve driving experience by providing critical information effortlessly.</p></p>
