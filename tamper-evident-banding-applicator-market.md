<p><p>The Tamper Evident Banding Applicator Market is categorized into two primary types: Full-Body Banding and Neck Banding. Full-Body Banding involves applying a tamper-evident band that encircles the entire product, ensuring comprehensive security and visibility of tampering. Neck Banding, on the other hand, applies a band around the neck of the product, typically used for bottles or jars, indicating tampering while allowing for easier access to the contents. Both types enhance product integrity and consumer safety.</p></p>
