<p><p>Cable entry systems are categorized into two main types: for cables without connectors and for cables with connectors. Systems designed for cables without connectors allow for direct entry of the cable into a housing, ensuring a secure seal while maintaining integrity. In contrast, systems for cables with connectors facilitate the entry of pre-terminated cables, accommodating the connector size and shape. Both types ensure protection against environmental factors and maintain effective cable management, crucial for various industrial and commercial applications.</p></p>
