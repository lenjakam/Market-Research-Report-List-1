<p><strong>The Implantable Ports Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Lumen Implantable Ports</li><li>Double-Lumen Implantable Ports</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The implantable ports market is primarily categorized into single-lumen and double-lumen implantable ports. Single-lumen ports consist of a single catheter that facilitates medication delivery or blood withdrawal through one channel, making them ideal for patients requiring limited access. In contrast, double-lumen ports have two separate lumens, allowing simultaneous access for different therapies, which is beneficial for patients needing multiple treatments. Both types improve patient care efficiency and comfort by minimizing the need for multiple needle insertions and enhancing long-term vascular access.</p></p>
