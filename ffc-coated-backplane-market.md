<p><p>The FFC coated backplane market is segmented into one-sided and double-sided coating types. One-sided coating involves applying a conductive layer to only one side of the backplane, offering cost-effectiveness and a lightweight solution for simpler applications. In contrast, double-sided coating provides enhanced electrical performance and durability by covering both sides, making it suitable for more complex devices requiring greater reliability and thermal management. The choice between these types depends on application requirements, performance needs, and design considerations.</p></p>
