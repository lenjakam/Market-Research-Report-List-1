<p><p>Distribution ERP software is essential for managing supply chains and streamlining operations. The market is primarily segmented into two types: on-premise and cloud-based solutions. On-premise ERP software is installed locally on a company’s servers, offering enhanced control and customization but requiring significant upfront investment and maintenance. Conversely, cloud-based ERP software is hosted on remote servers, providing flexibility, scalability, and easier access through the internet. This model typically involves subscription pricing, making it attractive for businesses seeking lower initial costs and quick deployment.</p></p>
