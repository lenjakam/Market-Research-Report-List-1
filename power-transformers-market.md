<p><ul><li>Dry-Type Transformer</li><li>Oil-Immersed Transformer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The power transformers market encompasses two primary types: dry-type and oil-immersed transformers. Dry-type transformers utilize air for cooling and insulation, making them suitable for indoor applications with reduced fire risk and lower maintenance requirements. Conversely, oil-immersed transformers use oil for cooling and insulation, allowing for higher efficiency and performance in larger power applications. Each type caters to different operational environments and customer needs, influencing their adoption in various sectors, including industrial, commercial, and utility applications.</p></p>
