<p><strong>The Hospital Ventilation System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Exhaust Ventilation Systems</li><li>Supply Ventilation Systems</li><li>Balanced Ventilation Systems</li><li>Energy Recovery Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hospital ventilation system market comprises several types: </p><p>1. **Exhaust Ventilation Systems** remove stale air and contaminants, ensuring a clean environment.  </p><p>2. **Supply Ventilation Systems** introduce fresh air, maintaining indoor air quality.  </p><p>3. **Balanced Ventilation Systems** utilize both supply and exhaust methods, promoting optimal air flow and comfort.  </p><p>4. **Energy Recovery Systems** efficiently transfer heat and moisture between incoming and outgoing air, reducing energy consumption.  </p><p>Together, these systems enhance patient safety and comfort in healthcare facilities.</p></p>
