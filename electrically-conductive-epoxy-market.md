<p><strong>The Electrically Conductive Epoxy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Isotropic</li><li>Anisotropic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electrically conductive epoxy market is categorized into two main types: isotropic and anisotropic. Isotropic conductive epoxy has uniform electrical properties in all directions, making it suitable for general applications, offering consistent performance in circuitry and bonding. Anisotropic conductive epoxy, on the other hand, displays directional conductivity, which allows it to conduct electricity primarily along a specific axis. This type is ideal for applications like chip-on-board connections, where precise electrical pathways are crucial, providing targeted connectivity and enhancing performance.</p></p>
