<p><p>Point-of-Entry Water Treatment Systems encompass various technologies designed to improve water quality at the source. Water softening reduces hardness minerals, enhancing soap efficiency. Filtration removes sediments and contaminants, ensuring clearer water. Reverse Osmosis (RO) effectively eliminates dissolved solids and impurities. Disinfection employs methods like UV light or chemical treatments to kill pathogens. Sedimentation allows particles to settle out by gravity, improving clarity. Distillation utilizes heat to vaporize water, separating it from impurities, yielding high-purity clean water.</p></p>
