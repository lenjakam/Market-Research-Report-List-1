<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The External Rotor Fan Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>External Rotor Axial Flow Fan</li><li>External Rotor Centrifugal Fan</li><li>External Rotor Diagonal Flow Fan</li><li>External Rotor Cross Flow Fan (Cross Flow Fan)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The external rotor fan market is categorized into four main types: </p><p>1. **External Rotor Axial Flow Fan** - Moves air parallel to the fan shaft, ideal for scenarios requiring high airflow with low pressure.</p><p>2. **External Rotor Centrifugal Fan** - Generates airflow perpendicular to the shaft, suitable for applications needing higher pressures.</p><p>3. **External Rotor Diagonal Flow Fan** - Combines features of axial and centrifugal fans, offering versatility in airflow direction.</p><p>4. **External Rotor Cross Flow Fan** - Creates airflow across the flow axis, commonly used in compact spaces for even air distribution.</p></p>
